# Skin

Skin is the third step of [[wiki/Techniques/Bear Hunter System|Bear Hunter System]]. It refines the rough structure created through [[wiki/Techniques/Aim|Aim]] and [[wiki/Techniques/Shoot|Shoot]] into a cleaner final form.

## Summary

Skin is a consolidation and judgment step. It asks: "Given everything I now understand, what structure best represents this topic?"

The goal is not to add more. The goal is to make the structure more accurate, intuitive, and retrievable.

## Cognitive Job

Skin should activate:

- Evaluation.
- Prioritization.
- Simplification.
- Chunk refinement.
- Relationship selection.
- Final structure judgment.

## Good Skin Behavior

- Remove weak or redundant links.
- Rename chunks so they reflect meaning.
- Reorganize around importance rather than source order.
- Make the map easier to reconstruct from memory.
- Decide which relationships deserve visual prominence.
- Convert the final structure into retrieval targets.

## Output

Skin should usually produce:

- A cleaned map.
- A final chunk structure.
- A concise topic backbone.
- High-quality retrieval prompts.
- A short list of remaining gaps.
