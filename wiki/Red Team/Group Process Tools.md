# Group Process Tools

Group process tools are Red Team methods for getting better thinking out of groups by reducing hierarchy effects and improving divergence before convergence.

## Design Principles

- Let people think alone before discussion.
- Protect dissent.
- Make rank less dominant.
- Use anonymity when status pressure is high.
- Separate idea generation from idea selection.
- Use structured convergence instead of letting the loudest voice win.

## Common Tools

- Think-Write-Share.
- Circle of Voices.
- Dot Voting.
- Team A / Team B.
- Fishbowl.
- 1-2-4-Whole Group.
- Troika Consulting.

## Solo Adaptation

For solo work with an LLM:

- First write your own answer.
- Ask the LLM for three opposing interpretations.
- Ask it to argue from a junior, adversary, maintainer, or outsider perspective.
- Ask it to list assumptions anonymously, meaning without attaching them to your preferred conclusion.
- Converge only after the alternatives are visible.
