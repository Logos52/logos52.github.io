# Creative Thinking

Creative Thinking in Red Teaming means generating ideas that are both novel and useful, especially when the existing frame is trapping the organization.

## Five-Stage Creative Thought Process

The handbook presents creative thought as iterative rather than perfectly linear:

1. **Problem-finding.** Look past symptoms and identify the real problem.
2. **Preparation.** Immerse in the problem, context, prior attempts, and related domains.
3. **Ideation.** Generate possibilities through divergence, incubation, and insight.
4. **Idea verification.** Stress test the new idea so the team does not fall in love with it.
5. **Communication.** Translate the idea so stakeholders understand it, support it, and can act on it.

## Red Team Contribution

Red Teaming helps creativity by forcing the organization to:

- Restate the problem.
- Challenge the analogy driving the plan.
- Generate multiple futures.
- Test the preferred idea.
- Think from stakeholder interests.
- Communicate the idea in a way the decision maker can use.
