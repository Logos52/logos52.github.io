# Communication Tools

Communication tools make Red Team analysis usable. A good critique that cannot influence the decision maker has not finished its job.

## Core Principle

Red Teaming is not only finding flaws. It is helping a leader or team make a better decision. The communication must be clear, timely, and tied to the decision.

## High-Value Tools

- [[wiki/Red Team/SEE-I|SEE-I]]
- Storytelling.
- Argument construction and deconstruction.
- Stakeholder-aware framing.
- Analogy suitability checks.

## Useful Standard

Good Red Team communication should answer:

- What is the issue?
- Why does it matter?
- What assumption or risk did we find?
- What evidence supports the concern?
- What alternative should the decision maker consider?
- What action is recommended?
