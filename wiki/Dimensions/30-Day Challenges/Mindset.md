# 30-Day Challenge – Mindset

## Week 1: Tracking Evidence of Improvement

Focus on collecting small, concrete evidence that improvement is happening. Track micro-wins, completed sessions, or moments of progress without judgment. The goal is to build a record of growth rather than relying on feelings.

## Week 2: Reframing Difficulty

When difficulty or resistance appears, practice shifting the internal question from “Am I capable?” to “What adjustment is needed?” Notice how the interpretation of struggle changes when the focus moves from self to process.

## Week 3: Generating Data Through Experiments

Run small, low-stakes experiments designed to produce visible results. Choose one variable to test each time and observe the outcome. Treat each attempt as data collection rather than a test of ability.

## Week 4: Reinforcing Growth Through Reflection

Review the month’s evidence and reflect on how ability has changed. Identify which experiments produced the strongest sense of progress and consider how to repeat or expand them.
