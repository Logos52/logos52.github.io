## 2. Marginal Metrics for Next Week

Set one or two specific, trackable targets for the coming week. Each metric should have a daily checkbox so progress is visible without opening the goals system.

| Metric | M | T | W | T | F | S | S |
|--------|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |
| | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ | ☐ |

**What makes a good marginal metric:**  
- Specific enough to check off at the end of the day (yes or no)
- Tied to one of the active processes above
- Small enough to be achievable in low-energy conditions

**Example:** "Complete one BHS Aim + Shoot block" — not "practise learning techniques"
