### Anchoring & Dissecting the Goal

**General direction:**  
Build Vietnamese through repeated contact with real input — immersion, noticing, and comprehension rather than grammar perfection.

**Medium term anchored goal:**  
By the end of 2026, be able to follow a native-speed conversation on a familiar topic with high comprehension, and produce simple spoken responses without pausing to translate.

#### What mindsets and attributes are needed?

| Mindset / Attribute | Current | Final | Notes |
|---------------------|---------|-------|-------|
| Patience with slow acquisition | 5/10 | 8/10 | Trusting the input-first process |
| Noticing over correcting | 4/10 | 8/10 | Observing patterns without immediately drilling rules |
| Contact recurrence | 4/10 | 8/10 | Daily low-friction touchpoints rather than intensive sessions |

#### What skills are needed?

| Skill | Current | Final | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|-------|
| Immersion Recurrence | 3/10 | 7/10 | Making contact with Vietnamese input a daily habit |
| Noticing | 3/10 | 6/10 | Spotting sounds, grammar patterns, repeated expressions |

#### Processes connected to this goal

- Watch or listen to Vietnamese input with full attention (daily or near-daily)
- Save useful phrases and expressions after each session
- Notice and name one pattern per session (sound, grammar, repeated expression)
- Rewatch short clips until they feel familiar before moving on
