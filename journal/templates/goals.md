## Process Checklist

The Pan / iCanStudy walkthrough. Work top-to-bottom. Not every goal needs every step.

- [ ] **Interior reflection**
  - [ ] Create a list of goals and dreams.
  - [ ] Write down what excites you most: activities where hours pass without noticing.
- [ ] **Exterior reflection**
  - [ ] Ask others about your strengths, weaknesses, what they admire, and what you need to improve.
  - [ ] Find similarities between interior and exterior reflection.
- [ ] **What is your general direction?**
- [ ] **Anchor the goal**: make it medium-term and pointed in that general direction.
- [ ] **Dissect the goal**: what skills, attributes, and mindsets does the perfect imaginary person who achieves this possess?
  - [ ] Fill in the dissection table below.
- [ ] **Add the new skills, attributes, and mindsets to the [[journal/templates/skills-index|Skills Index]]**
  - [ ] Rate where I am now vs. where I need to be.
  - [ ] Set the anchored goal on each skill.
- [ ] **Mindset planning**: how will I keep my mindset in check while developing these?
- [ ] **Choose the most important skills** and set SMARTER performance goals.
  - [ ] Use top-down and bottom-up approaches.
  - [ ] Keep 7-day and 14-day performance goals easy enough to hit on bad weeks.
- [ ] **Create an accountability board of four people.**
- [ ] **Create a list of potential mentors** and connect with them.
- [ ] **Schedule reviews of this page.**
- [ ] **Schedule Priority 0 reviews.**
- [ ] Optional: schedule 30-day plans.
